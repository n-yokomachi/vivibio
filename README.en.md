# vivibio

> **vivid** × **vivere** × **bio** — A digital business card for vibrant lives

vivibio is a personal introduction website that consolidates who you are into a single web page.
Centered around links to SNS profiles and publishing platforms, it is designed to be written onto an NFC card and shared as a modern alternative to paper business cards.

[日本語 README](./README.md)

---

## ✨ Concept

- **vivid** — A profile that leaves a vivid impression
- **vivere** — Latin for "to live"; conveying who you are right now
- **bio** — Background and persona, kept simple

Unlike a portfolio site, vivibio is not a showcase of works.
It functions as **a link hub for the person themselves**.
By writing the site URL onto an NFC card (similar to Prairie Card), it offers a fresh self-introduction experience that replaces traditional paper business cards.

## 📦 Planned Features

- Profile section (name, title, avatar, bio)
- SNS link hub (X, GitHub, Zenn, etc.)
- Links to published articles
- Contact and work inquiry information
- QR code display (for on-the-spot scanning)
- Dark / light theme toggle
- Multilingual support (Japanese / English)

## 📡 Distribution

This site is designed to be written onto an NFC card (such as Prairie Card) and shared as a digital business card.
A simple tap with a smartphone opens vivibio, allowing the recipient to instantly follow your SNS or grab your contact information.
